Express Interest

Before you start doing anything significant you should express your interest by letting us know what you wish to accomplish. We will try and provide you with:

  • The proper context to work in.
  • The APIs you need to know about.
  • The constraints you must honor.
  • An asessment about where your contribution fits in today and where it will fit in in future.

Your questions and our feedback will be listed publicly for others to learn from. We aim to make you as efficient as possible so you may get to contributing on a regular basis.
